Help & Instructions 🆘

Default Timer: 30 seconds (adjustable in Settings ⚙️)

🎮 Play Mode

  • Click a question to open it — the countdown timer starts automatically.
  • Use Reveal Answer 👀 to show or hide the answer manually.
  • Click + Team or − Team to adjust points.
  • Used questions are grayed out until you reset the board.

✏️ Edit Mode

  • Click “Switch to Edit Mode ✏️” to modify any question, answer, or category title.
  • Changes are saved automatically in your browser.
  • Editing a question resets its used state.

💾 Save & Export

  • Save / Export 💾 — downloads all your custom questions and answers as a .json file.
  • Import 📂 — upload that file on another computer to restore your game setup.

⚙️ Settings

  • Change auto-reveal delay (set to 0 for manual reveal).
  • Adjust question timer duration (default 30 seconds).
  • Settings are saved automatically.

➕ Manual Score Buttons

  • Use the + and − beside each team’s score to manually change totals (+100 / −100).
